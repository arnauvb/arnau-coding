Football signings VS Foreign Aid

By Arnau Vila Buch

Follow @arnauvilabuch

Spain is the second country in the world, after the United Kingdom, that spends more money on football. Spanish football teams pay multi-million signings for new football players to join their teams.

An exclusive analysis of football expenditure shows that not the same amount of money is invested in foreign aid. In fact, there is a heated debate as many people believe that aid levels are too low, and if more money was spent on this issue, the worldwide poverty would be significantly reduced.
